Save money with eXtrabuck

Our application let's you save up to 15% money whenever you shop for basically anything in more then 50 countries worldwide. 15 million people are already getting their benefits everyday by simply showing member id number at the cashier. We are not giving you flowers, butterflies, smileys, points or anything similar, you will get live cash back to your account. Once you gather the amount you want, you can simply send your cash from the application to your bank account. You can as well spend the cash from app directly to buy vouchers and pay with them at the cashier. For online shopping, just simply open the online store webpage from the list in the application, and make sure you enable cookies in your browser. That way you will get your benefits from the most popular online stores in the world.


How is this even possible?

Simply, our local and online partner stores are not paying for the old-fashioned marketing campaigns since they do not always bring customers to their business. They save enormous amounts of money by getting their loyalty program active and they can easily do that by partnering up with us. These stores are as well earning money from all the other stores where their registered members shop. It is a win-win situation for both customers and stores selling their products or services. Customers get their benefits easily and stores don't have to pay for marketing unless they make sales and earn profit.
