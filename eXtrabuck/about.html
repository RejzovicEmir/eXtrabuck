What is eXtrabuck brand?

eXtrabuck brand started operating in September 2020 with the main aim of letting people all around the world know alternative ways of saving money. We were mainly inspired to do this by the Covid-19 pandemic that overtook our whole planet which may as well cause some very serious economic crisis. Our services are 100% free of any charge and currently work in more then 50 countries worldwide.Each year we have more and more countries joining our system and expanding network.


What is it all about?

It's about shopping, 2nd most frequent occurrence in the world after breathing. Our brand shows you a free iOS/android application which lets you save money whenever you shop for products or services you need. You would pay for these anyway, wouldn't you? This way you can save up to 15% money back, each time you shop. It works for both local and online shopping, giving you your benefits every time.


We raise money for our Ecology and Charity Foundations!

Each time you shop, the store pays 1% of total money spent to our Foundations. It is then equally divided into our Ecology and Charity Foundation.
